Faculty End of Year: SquidwArt and Lab Scouring Day!

Published: 7-7-'24/ Last updated: 7-7-'24
Written by Milan
SquidwArt competition piece displayed in the city, lab cleaning day.

As is usual at Squidward Community College, the headmaster, Squidward himself, has organised an art competition among the pupils, namely the SquidwArt. SquidwArt is a prestigious art competition in which, historically, only students at the Art faculty were able to get invloved with. Nowadays, there are actually two competitions: one only students could enter and a new one where anyone can participate. Furthermore, the vying may not be limited to visual art. Musicians are also encouraged to come through. However, in the category of painting, students' works are displayed around the city, thus everyone is able to take pleasure in this competition and its beautiful artworks. Milan and his step mom took a closer look at these oeuvres.

Cleaning the lab is last on the to-do list. This year, the institute decided to let the first years suffer by letting them clean the lab. Those who did not come to the lab cleaning day are expected to come back in September. To clean of course.
