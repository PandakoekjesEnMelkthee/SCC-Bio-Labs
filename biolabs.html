Research & Education

Welcome to Squidward Community College Bio Labs!

SCC Bio Labs is part of the faculty of Natural Sciences and is to a large extent connected to the courses Biology and Chemistry. Students and researchers from SCC thus have access to modern laboratories and present-day equipment in molecular laboratory techniques. Aside from education, we are best known in academia for our world class research on topics such as microbiology and biomedicine which is conducted in association with leading scientists. Being a leader in research therefore allows students to learn in an experiment-oriented fashion which brings their theoretical education into practice. Moreover, research is conducted with private companies on matters like business administration.

On this site you will find news and articles on the latest research taking place at SCC Bio Labs (and to a slight extent at the faculty of Natural Sciences) and information for students who are doing an internship or taking a course in i.e. Biochemistry or Cell Physiology at SCC Bio Labs.
